About Me
I was born in small town America in the early 80s. I grew up loving computers and technology, though I never pursued it proffessionaly as a career. I moved to India in my 20's where I attended University and studied the Hindi Language and linguistics. While in India I met my wife. We have one child. During my time in India I have been invovled in several buisness and social ventures. Currently I provide high quality Hindi Courses online through HindiUnlocked and am invovled in marketing and managing for my wifes handicraft export company. I created this website as an assignment for CS50w at EDx. I thought it would be an good opportunity to showcase some of my work and share some of my journey as a coder. I hope you have enjoyed it.
